Description

If you enjoy pancakes for breakfast, lunch and dinner, you will love this dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch green onions chopped
  • 1 chunk cabbage grated
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 1/4 cup of rice flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon msg
  • 1 teaspoon chilli powder
  • 2 eggs, beaten

Steps

  • Chop the green onions, grate the cabbage and place in a bowl
  • Mince the garlic and add to the bowl with the cabbage and green onion
  • In a seperate bowl, beat 2 eggs and set aside
  • Add your msg and chilli powder to the eggs
  • After you have added the seasoning, add the flour and mix until there are no lumps
  • Spoon in the cabbage, green onions, mixture and fold
  • Spoon pancakes onto a cast iron skillet and cook for 20 minutes, flipping every 2 to 3 minutes
  • Serve with soy sauce and enjoy!
